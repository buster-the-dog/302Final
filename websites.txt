All of the following are useful websites that could be used to scrape data

2019 Stats: Notice all URLs are the same besides posID
QBs - https://fftoday.com/stats/playerstats.php?Season=2019&GameWeek=&PosID=10&LeagueID=26955
- Name, Team, Games, 
- PassCompletions, PassAttempts, Yards, TDs, INTs,
- RushAttempts, RushYards, RushTDs, 
- FantasyPoints, FantasyPoints/Game

RBs - https://fftoday.com/stats/playerstats.php?Season=2019&GameWeek=&PosID=20&LeagueID=26955
- Name, Team, Games, 
- RushAtt, RushYard, RushTDs
- RecTarget, Receptions, RecYards, RecTDs
- FantasyPoints, FantasyPoints/Game

WRs - https://fftoday.com/stats/playerstats.php?Season=2019&GameWeek=&PosID=30&LeagueID=26955
- Name, Team, Games,
- RecTarget, Receptions, RecYards, RecTDs
- RushAtt, RushYard, RushTDs
- FantasyPoints, FantasyPoints/Game

TEs - https://fftoday.com/stats/playerstats.php?Season=2019&GameWeek=&PosID=40&LeagueID=26955
- Name, Team, Games,
- RecTarget, Receptions, RecYards, RecTDs
- FantasyPoints, FantasyPoints/Game

Ks  - https://fftoday.com/stats/playerstats.php?Season=2019&GameWeek=&PosID=80&LeagueID=26955
- Name, Team, Games,
- FGsMade, FGsAttempted, FG%, PATsMade, PATsAttempted,
- FantasyPoints, FantasyPoints/Game

DEF - https://fftoday.com/stats/playerstats.php?Season=2019&GameWeek=&PosID=99&LeagueID=26955
- Team, Games,
- Sacks, FumbleRecovery, INTs, DefTDs, 
- PointsAgainst, PassYDS/Game, RushYards/Game, Safetys, KickTDs
- FantasyPoints, FantasyPoints/Game

There are also different defensive positions such as LBs and DBs, don't know if we wanna include those
